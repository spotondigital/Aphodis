Microbial Media — Precision & Ease in Every Format

Our microbial media portfolio is designed for efficiency, reliability, and versatility—making lab workflows smoother and outcomes more dependable. Explore our range:

1. Ready-to-Use Agar Plates (Solidified)

Pre-poured and sterile, our agar plates are available in:

  • 50 mm, 65 mm, and 90 mm Petri dishes, ideal for routine culturing and colony isolation.
  • General-purpose media like Nutrient Agar and Tryptic Soy Agar (TSA), as well as specialized differential or selective media (e.g., Blood, MacConkey, Sabouraud).

Benefits:

  • No preparation—simply open and use.
  • Consistent quality batch-to-batch.
  • Perfect for colony morphology studies, microbial counts, and purity assays.

2. Ready-to-Use Liquid Agar (Semi-Solid/Solid Media)

Pre-sterilized liquid agar format in 250 mL bottles, ready to dispense into tubes or pour your own plates.

  • Useful for re-melting into plates or preparing slants and deeps.
  • Based on standard agar compositions (1–2% agar), derived from red algae.

3. Dehydrated Agar Powder Media

Premium agar in powdered or granular form—ideal for labs that prefer customized media prep.

  • Includes classic media: Nutrient Agar, Plate Count Agar, Potato Dextrose Agar, TSA.
  • Shelf-stable for up to 2 years—just add water, autoclave, and pour plates or tubes.

4. Broths (Liquid Media)

Our liquid media selection supports both enrichment and general culturing needs:

  • Nutrient Broth, Tryptic Soy Broth, Thioglycollate Broth—supporting aerobic, anaerobic, and fastidious organisms.
  • Available in ready-to-use bottles or dehydrated powder format.

5. Swabs & Transport Media

Specialized sterile swabs pre-packaged in transport media for accurate sampling:

  • Includes Stuart’s, Amies, Cary–Blair types—especially suited for clinical, environmental, and food/beverage sampling.

6. Supplements & Enrichment Additives

Enhance your media with supplements—blood, serum, antibiotics, growth factors:

  • Used for selective or enriched growth of fastidious microbes (e.g., Blood, Chocolate, Thayer-Martin agars).
