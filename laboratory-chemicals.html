Laboratory Chemicals & Analytical Reagents

Comprehensive Solutions in Every Size

We understand the diverse needs of modern laboratories—from R&D to quality control, education to industrial testing. That's why we offer a wide spectrum of laboratory chemicals and analytical reagents, available in various purity grades and packaged in multiple volumes to suit every workflow.

Chemicals & Reagents

  • Acids & Bases
    Fundamental for titrations, pH adjustments, and neutralization reactions. Examples include hydrochloric, sulfuric, and nitric acids; sodium hydroxide and ammonium hydroxide for bases.
  • Solvents
    Essential for dissolving and extracting compounds or cleaning equipment. Common solvents include ethanol, acetone, methanol, hexane, and dichloromethane (DCM).
  • Buffers & Indicators
    Maintain stable pH (e.g., Tris buffer) and reveal reaction endpoints (e.g., phenolphthalein, bromothymol blue).
  • Oxidizing & Reducing Agents
    Examples include potassium permanganate, hydrogen peroxide (oxidizers), and sodium borohydride, lithium aluminum hydride (reducers).
  • Precipitants & Complexing Reagents
    Silver nitrate and ammonium hydroxide help form precipitates; EDTA and dimethylglyoxime enable metal ion complexing.
  • Analytical Standards
    High-purity reference materials used for calibration in chromatography, spectrophotometry, and other analyses.
  • Specialty Reagents
    Organic and inorganic reagents including Fehling’s, Tollen’s, and Millon’s—commonly used for qualitative analysis.
